DOCUMENT REVIEW SCHEDULE

The following document categories are reviewed and updated annually:

- Time Off Policy: January
- Benefits Guide: January (after open enrollment is finalized)
- Code of Conduct: January
- IT & Equipment Policy: January
- Expense Policy: February
- Remote Work Policy: March

Ad hoc updates are made whenever a policy changes mid-year. The Digital Ops team coordinates with HR and IT to keep documents current.

If you notice an outdated document in Nexus, flag it in the #nexus-feedback Slack channel.



VENDOR NOTES

Current Nexus infrastructure:
- Vector database: ChromaDB (hosted internally)
- Embedding model: BAAI/bge-small-en-v1.5
- LLM: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
- Hosting: Internal Kubernetes cluster

Evaluating for future consideration:
- Pinecone (managed vector DB) — would reduce maintenance overhead but adds cost
- Moving to a larger embedding model for improved retrieval accuracy on short queries
- Adding metadata filtering so users can restrict searches to a specific document category (e.g., "only show me HR policies")
