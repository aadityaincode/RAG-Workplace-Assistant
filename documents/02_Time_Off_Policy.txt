TIME OFF POLICY — ARROWOOD & PARTNERS
Last Updated: January 2025



VACATION DAYS

Full-time employees accrue vacation days based on their tenure with the company:

- Year 1: 15 days per year (accrued monthly)
- Years 2–4: 18 days per year
- Year 5 and beyond: 22 days per year

Part-time employees (working 20+ hours per week) accrue vacation at 50% of the above rates.

Vacation days do not expire at year-end. You may carry over up to 10 unused days into the following calendar year. Any days above 10 are forfeited on January 1.



PUBLIC HOLIDAYS

All employees receive the following paid public holidays each year:

- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day (US) / Family Day (Canada)
- Memorial Day (US) / Victoria Day (Canada)
- Independence Day (US) / Canada Day (Canada)
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (US: November | Canada: October)
- Christmas Day
- Boxing Day (Toronto office only)

In addition, employees receive 2 floating holidays per year, which can be used for personal observances, religious holidays, or any day of their choosing. Floating holidays do not carry over.



SICK LEAVE

Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year. Sick days reset on January 1 and do not carry over. Sick leave is separate from vacation and may be used for personal illness, caring for a sick immediate family member, or medical appointments.

If you are unwell for 3 or more consecutive days, you may be asked to provide a doctor's note. Long-term illness beyond 10 days is handled under our Short-Term Disability policy — contact HR for details.



BEREAVEMENT LEAVE

Employees may take the following paid bereavement leave following the death of a family member:

- Immediate family (spouse, child, parent, sibling): 5 days
- Extended family (grandparent, in-law, aunt, uncle): 3 days
- Close friend or colleague (at manager's discretion): 1 day

Additional unpaid time may be granted at the manager's discretion.



PARENTAL LEAVE

Primary caregiver: 16 weeks fully paid
Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks fully paid

Employees must have been with the company for at least 12 months to be eligible for paid parental leave. Shorter-tenure employees may take unpaid parental leave in accordance with applicable law.

To initiate parental leave, notify your manager and HR at least 8 weeks in advance where possible.



HOW TO REQUEST TIME OFF

All time off requests are submitted through the HR portal at hr.arrowood.internal. Requests should be submitted at least:

- 2 weeks in advance for vacation of 3+ days
- 24 hours in advance for single vacation days (subject to manager approval)

Sick leave does not require advance notice. Send a message to your manager and team before your scheduled start time on any day you are taking sick leave.
