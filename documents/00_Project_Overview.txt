Project Name: Nexus Internal Knowledge Base
Version: 2.1.0
Status: Active
Owner: Digital Operations Team

OVERVIEW
Nexus is the internal knowledge platform for Arrowood & Partners, a mid-sized professional services firm with offices in Austin, Chicago, and Toronto. It allows employees to search company documents, policies, and guidelines using plain English questions instead of digging through shared drives.
The system was introduced in Q1 2025 after a company-wide survey found that employees were spending an average of 90 minutes per day trying to locate internal information. Nexus reduced that to under 15 minutes within the first quarter of rollout.

WHO IT'S FOR
Nexus is available to all full-time and part-time employees. It is particularly used by:
- New hires during onboarding, who need to quickly get up to speed on company policies
- Operations staff handling day-to-day HR and admin questions
- Project managers looking for process documentation
- Client services teams referencing internal guidelines

WHAT YOU CAN ASK
You can ask Nexus questions like:
- "How many vacation days do I get in my first year?"
- "What is the expense reimbursement process?"
- "Who do I contact for IT issues?"
- "What are the guidelines for remote work?"
Nexus will provide an answer and show you which document it pulled the information from so you can read the full source if needed.
