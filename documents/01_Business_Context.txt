Business Problem:

Employees spend an average of 2.3 hours per day searching for internal information across multiple systems.

This leads to:
- Reduced productivity
- Duplicate work
- Increased onboarding time

ProjectX solves this by:

1. Centralizing internal knowledge
2. Making documents searchable using semantic meaning
3. Providing instant answers

Expected benefits:

- 40% reduction in knowledge retrieval time
- Faster onboarding
- Reduced support escalations
