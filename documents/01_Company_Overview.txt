ARROWOOD & PARTNERS — COMPANY OVERVIEW

Founded: 2009
Headquarters: Austin, Texas
Additional Offices: Chicago, Illinois | Toronto, Ontario
Employees: ~430 globally
Industry: Professional Services (Management Consulting & Operations Advisory)



WHAT WE DO

Arrowood & Partners helps mid-market companies improve how they operate. Our core service areas are:

- Operations Advisory: Streamlining business processes and reducing inefficiencies
- Change Management: Supporting organizations through restructuring and transitions
- Technology Integration: Helping clients adopt new tools without disrupting day-to-day work
- Talent Strategy: Workforce planning, org design, and leadership development

Most of our clients are in healthcare, financial services, and logistics. Engagements typically run between 3 and 18 months.



OUR VALUES

We operate by four principles that guide how we work with clients and each other:

1. Clarity — We communicate simply and honestly, even when the message is difficult.
2. Ownership — We take responsibility for outcomes, not just effort.
3. Respect — We treat clients, colleagues, and partners with consistent professionalism.
4. Curiosity — We ask better questions before jumping to solutions.
