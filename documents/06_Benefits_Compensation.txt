BENEFITS & COMPENSATION GUIDE — ARROWOOD & PARTNERS
Last Updated: January 2025



SALARY & PAYROLL

Employees are paid bi-weekly (every two weeks) via direct deposit. The payroll schedule for the year is published in the HR portal at the start of each January.

Salary reviews take place annually in January. Adjustments, if any, are reflected in the February 1 paycheck. Mid-year adjustments may occur for promotions or significant role changes.



HEALTH INSURANCE (US EMPLOYEES)

Arrowood & Partners covers 85% of the monthly premium for employee health insurance and 65% for dependents. Three plan tiers are available:

- Base Plan (HSA-compatible)
- Standard Plan (PPO)
- Premium Plan (PPO with lower deductible)

Dental and vision coverage are included in all plans at no additional premium for the employee.

Open enrollment takes place each November for coverage beginning January 1. New hires may enroll within 30 days of their start date.

For questions about your health plan, contact benefits@arrowood.internal.



HEALTH INSURANCE (CANADA EMPLOYEES — TORONTO OFFICE)

Canadian employees are enrolled in the company's group benefits plan through Sun Life Financial. Coverage includes:

- Extended health (prescriptions, paramedical, vision)
- Dental (basic and major)
- Life insurance (2x annual salary)
- Short-term and long-term disability

The company covers 80% of premiums for the employee. Dependent coverage is available at the employee's cost.



RETIREMENT / PENSION

US Employees — 401(k):
Arrowood matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of base salary. Employees are eligible to participate immediately upon hire. Vesting is immediate for matched contributions.

Canadian Employees — RRSP:
The company contributes 4% of base salary to a group RRSP on behalf of the employee, regardless of whether the employee contributes. Employees may make additional personal contributions up to their CRA limit.



WELLNESS BENEFIT

Every employee receives a $400 annual wellness allowance (USD or CAD depending on location). This can be used for:

- Gym or fitness memberships
- Mental health apps (Calm, Headspace, etc.)
- Therapy or counseling (if not fully covered by health plan)
- Fitness equipment
- Recreational sports leagues or classes

Submit receipts through the HR portal. The wellness allowance resets January 1 and does not carry over.



EMPLOYEE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM (EAP)

All employees and their immediate family members have access to the Employee Assistance Program, which provides free, confidential support for:

- Mental health counseling (up to 8 sessions per year at no cost)
- Financial planning advice
- Legal consultations
- Caregiver and eldercare resources

The EAP is operated by a third-party provider. Arrowood does not receive any information about individual use. Access through: eap.arrowood.internal or call 1-800-555-0182 (24/7).



PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Each employee has an annual learning budget of $1,500 USD / $2,000 CAD for:

- Courses, certifications, and workshops
- Conference registration fees
- Professional memberships
- Books and learning resources

Unused professional development budget does not carry over. Requests over $500 require manager approval before purchase.



REFERRAL BONUS

Employees who refer a candidate who is successfully hired and completes 6 months of employment receive a referral bonus of $2,000 USD / $2,600 CAD (paid after the 6-month mark). Referrals must be submitted through the HR portal before the candidate applies.
