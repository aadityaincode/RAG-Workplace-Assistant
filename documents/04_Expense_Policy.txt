EXPENSE & REIMBURSEMENT POLICY — ARROWOOD & PARTNERS
Last Updated: February 2025



OVERVIEW

Arrowood & Partners reimburses employees for reasonable, work-related expenses incurred while doing their job. All expenses must be submitted with a receipt and approved by your direct manager. Expenses submitted without receipts will not be reimbursed.



TRAVEL

Flights:
Book economy class for all domestic and short-haul international travel (under 5 hours). Business class may be approved for flights over 6 hours with prior written approval from your department head.

Book through the company travel portal at travel.arrowood.internal where possible. Personal bookings are reimbursable but must be comparable in price to what the portal would have offered.

Hotels:
The nightly hotel limit is:
- Austin / Chicago / Toronto: $220 USD / $280 CAD
- New York / San Francisco / London: $300 USD / $380 CAD
- Other cities: $180 USD / $230 CAD

If the only available options exceed these limits, contact your manager before booking.

Ground Transportation:
Taxis, rideshares (Uber, Lyft), and public transit are all reimbursable. Rental cars require pre-approval unless no other reasonable option exists. Personal vehicle mileage is reimbursed at the current IRS rate (US) or CRA rate (Canada).



MEALS & ENTERTAINMENT

Meals while traveling:
- Breakfast: up to $20
- Lunch: up to $35
- Dinner: up to $65

Client entertainment (dinners, events):
Up to $100 per person per event. The employee hosting must be present. Submit the names of all attendees and the business purpose along with the receipt.

Team lunches or office meals ordered for a group require manager pre-approval. There is a budget of $25 per person for approved team lunches.



CONFERENCES & PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

Registration fees for approved industry conferences are fully covered. You must receive written approval from your manager before registering.

Books, online courses, and certifications are reimbursable up to $500 USD per employee per year under the Professional Development budget. Submit receipts within 60 days of purchase.



TECHNOLOGY & EQUIPMENT

Hardware (laptops, monitors, accessories) is purchased by IT and issued to employees. Do not purchase your own work laptop and expect reimbursement unless explicitly authorized by IT.

Software subscriptions needed for your work must be approved by IT before purchase. Unauthorized software subscriptions will not be reimbursed.



HOW TO SUBMIT AN EXPENSE

1. Go to the expense portal at expenses.arrowood.internal
2. Create a new expense report and attach your receipts (photo or PDF)
3. Select the appropriate expense category
4. Add a brief description of the business purpose
5. Submit to your manager for approval

Expense reports should be submitted within 30 days of the expense. Reports older than 90 days may be declined.

Approved expenses are reimbursed via direct deposit on the next regular payroll cycle after approval.



QUESTIONS

For questions about whether a specific expense is covered, contact finance@arrowood.internal before making the purchase.
