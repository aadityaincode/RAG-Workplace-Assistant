IT & EQUIPMENT POLICY — ARROWOOD & PARTNERS
Last Updated: January 2025



EQUIPMENT ISSUED TO EMPLOYEES

All full-time employees receive the following equipment on their first day:

- MacBook Pro (14" or 16" depending on role)
- USB-C hub and power adapter
- Company-issued phone (client-facing roles only)

If your role requires additional equipment (external monitor, drawing tablet, specialty headset), your manager submits a request to IT before your start date. Equipment requests after onboarding go through the IT request portal at it.arrowood.internal.

All equipment remains the property of Arrowood & Partners and must be returned in good condition upon departure.



SOFTWARE & TOOLS

Standard tools installed on all machines:

- Slack (internal communication)
- Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook)
- Zoom (video conferencing)
- 1Password (password management)
- Notion (project documentation and knowledge base)
- Okta (single sign-on)

You may request additional software through the IT portal. Requests are reviewed within 3 business days. Do not download or install software on your work machine without IT approval. Unauthorized software may create security vulnerabilities and will be flagged during routine audits.



PASSWORDS & ACCOUNT SECURITY

All employees must use 1Password to generate and store passwords. Do not reuse passwords across accounts.

Password requirements for company accounts:
- Minimum 14 characters
- Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Never share your password with anyone, including IT staff

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all company accounts. MFA is set up via Okta during onboarding. If you lose access to your MFA device, contact IT immediately at it-support@arrowood.internal.



DATA & FILE STORAGE

All work files must be stored in company-approved locations:

- SharePoint / OneDrive: Documents, reports, presentations
- Notion: Project notes, wikis, internal documentation
- Secure client folders: Client deliverables (access controlled by project)

Do not store work files on your local machine only. Do not use personal Google Drive, Dropbox, or iCloud for work documents.



LOST OR DAMAGED EQUIPMENT

Report lost or stolen equipment to IT within 24 hours at it-support@arrowood.internal. IT will remotely wipe the device if necessary.

If equipment is damaged due to normal wear, IT will arrange a replacement. Damage due to negligence (e.g., dropping an unprotected laptop) may result in a partial cost recovery — IT will assess on a case-by-case basis.



IT SUPPORT

For technical issues, contact IT through:

- Slack: #it-support channel
- Email: it-support@arrowood.internal
- Portal: it.arrowood.internal

Response times:
- Critical (system down, security incident): within 1 hour
- High (unable to work): within 4 hours
- Normal (general issues): within 1 business day
- Low (requests, upgrades): within 3 business days
